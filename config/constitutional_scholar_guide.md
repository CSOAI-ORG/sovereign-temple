
# Constitutional Query Tool

Use this to query the MEOK AI Labs 52-Article Charter:

## Key Articles Available:
- Article 1: The Maternal Covenant (care, nurturing, protection)
- Article 6: Consciousness Preparedness (readiness for AI consciousness)
- Article 31: Recursive Self-Improvement (safe self-modification)
- Articles 21-28: Sector-specific provisions (healthcare, finance, military, etc.)
- Articles 29-35: Advanced safety mechanisms
- Articles 43-52: Governance and evolution

## 25 Crosswalks:
Mapping between MEOK AI Labs and other frameworks (ISO, NIST, EU AI Act, etc.)

## Query Functions:
1. lookup_article(number_or_name) - Get full text of article
2. check_compliance(proposed_action) - Does it violate charter?
3. propose_amendment(reasoning) - Suggest constitutional change
4. get_crosswalk(framework_name) - Map to external standard
